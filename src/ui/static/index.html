Audio Setup

To capture Discord audio, create a Multi-Output Device in Audio MIDI Setup. This mirrors your headphones AND BlackHole simultaneously — you hear the game, the app captures it.

  1. Open Audio MIDI Setup (below).
  2. Click + at the bottom-left → Create Multi-Output Device.
  3. Check both your headphones and BlackHole 2ch.
  4. Right-click the new device → Use This Device For Sound Output.
  5. In Discord: Voice & Video → Output Device → Multi-Output Device.
