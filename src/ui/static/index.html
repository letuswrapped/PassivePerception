Welcome to Passive Perception

Your personal D&D session scribe. It listens to your game, transcribes everything, identifies who's speaking, and writes your campaign notes automatically.

Let's get you set up in a few quick steps.

Set Up Audio Routing

The BlackHole audio driver was installed with the app. Now you need to create a Multi-Output Device so you can hear your game and the app can capture the audio.

1
Open Audio MIDI Setup
Applications → Utilities → Audio MIDI Setup
2
Click the + button at the bottom-left and select "Create Multi-Output Device"
3
Check both your headphones/speakers AND BlackHole 2ch
4
Right-click the new device → "Use This Device For Sound Output"
5
In Discord Settings → Voice → Output Device → select "Multi-Output Device"
⚔️
You're All Set!

Here's how it works:

1
Select "BlackHole 2ch" as the audio input device in the top bar
2
Name your session (e.g. "Session 14 — The Dragon's Lair")
3
Click "Begin Session" — the transcript and notes update live as you play
4
When you're done, click "End Session" and export your notes

Roll for initiative. The scribe is listening.
